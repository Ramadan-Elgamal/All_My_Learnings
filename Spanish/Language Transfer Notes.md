## Video 2
Spanish is like a street version of Latin. It is very similar to English. You identify Latin words in English and turn them to their Spanish vocabs very easily. Examples:
- Words ending with `al` in English is the same in Spanish with difference in the Pronounciation of course. ( normal -> normal )
- the verb `is` is converted to `es`.
- every letter is pronounced as it is not like in English ( there is no cha sound ).
